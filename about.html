About this APP
This is not an official UCR app and is not affiliated with UCR, GoHighlander is made by UCR Students.

This app is inspired by Bruinwalk and GoGaucho made by students in other UC schools.

Some information may not be shown correctly, etc. Please use button send feedback if you see anything wrong.
Privacy Notice
This application does not collect or store any personal information from its users. Helper pages are static and do not require any form of user authentication.

External links provided under "Useful Links" lead to third-party websites and do not route through this application. Location services are used solely to calculate the distance between your location and the destination. We do not record or store your location. If you prefer, you may disable location services, which will only affect the distance display functionality.

Cookies are used solely to store course information when you add courses to your schedule. All course data is saved locally on your device and is not transmitted to or stored on our server. This application does not maintain a database or store user data.

Your class schedule and UCR email are deleted from our system immediately after the QR-Code email is sent. We do not retain any of your information on our server. If you are uncomfortable with these terms, you can manually add courses instead.

By using this application, you acknowledge and accept these terms. If you have concerns, please discontinue use or disable specific features as described.
